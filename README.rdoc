== University of Scouting : Geocaching Challenge

This simple contest to help encourage registration for the University of Scouting (Feb 9, 2013). The contest should go online as soon as possible.

= Structure

The contestant should:
1. Set up their account (email and password) or login to the site.
2. Enter their answers to 24 Merit Badge trivia questions.
3. Return to the site to update their answers.
